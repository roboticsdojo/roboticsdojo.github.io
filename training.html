Bootcamp

The bootcamp training will be held from mid August until the end of September to accelerate the robot development toward the competition.

The bootcamp training will be provided for the team who participate in the competition. The participation by an individual is not accepted.

The tentative contents and schedule are shown below. All of the topic are essential to solve the challenges in the competition.

  1. Assembly of robot kit
  2. Design of manipulator mechanism
  3. Line following technique
  4. Ultrasonic sensor for obstacle avoidance
  5. Image filtering using Python, OpenCV, and Raspberry Pi
  6. Network programming to acquire image from livestreaming server
  7. Shortest-path planning algorithm
