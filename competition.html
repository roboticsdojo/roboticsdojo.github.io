Competition

Here below is the tentative summary of the competition 2022.

(The content in this page will be frequently updated. Check the latest infomation.)

Date 18th October 2022 (Qualifying round)
19th October 2022 (Championship)
Venue iPIC building, JKUAT (TBD)
Number of teams 10 (Qualifying round)
4 (Championship)
Eligibility of teams A team must comprise at least three members and can have five members at maximum (i.e. 3≤N≤5)
Eligibility of members A team member must be the current student of JKUAT or PAUSTI

Regulation of robot

Type of robot Either kit or custom-made
Number of wheels Unlimited
Number of motors Up to 4 motors
Dimension Smaller than 30cm x 30cm x 30 cm (W x D x H)
Weight Up to 2 kg
Controller board Arduino compatible boards (custom board allowed) and Raspberry Pi boards
Operation Fully autonomous

Regulation of race

The robot is supposed tackle four challenges in the playfield.

  1. Line following on circuit
  2. Obstacle avoidance
  3. Pause & resume
  4. Pick & place

Each team's score is evaluated by the performance in addressing each challenge and the time required to complete the race.
