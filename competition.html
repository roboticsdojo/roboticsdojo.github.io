Robotics Dojo Competition 2023

Date 4th October 2023
Venue Exhibition hall, iPIC building, JKUAT
Number of teams 9+2 (2 are JKUAT ROBOKEN teams)
Eligibility of teams A team must comprise at least three members and can have five members at maximum (i.e. 3≤N≤5)
Eligibility of members A team member must be the current student of JKUAT or PAUSTI

Rules of competition

The rule for the ROBOKEN 2023 is applied for the competition.

See the ROBOKEN 2023 RULES (PDF).

Timeline

• Team entry deadline: 24th July 2023

• Notification of accepted teams: 25th July 2023

• Orientation (Online meeting): 26th July 2023

• Instruction for robot design: end of July to early August

• Submission of budget plan: 9th August 2023

• Competition: 4th October 2023

The details will be updated on the Google slides: Robotics Dojo 2023 guidance
