About Robotics Dojo

Welcome to the Robotics Dojo. This is an intensive robotics training program that will enhance your knowledge and skill for developing robots.

Through the project-based activity and training, the participants will be able to strengthen their ability to develop robots and conduct robotics research.

The second JKUAT robotic competition is held in October 2023.

Our approach

Our training is a project-based program, where the students will be able to learn the basics of robotics by developings robots for the competition.
