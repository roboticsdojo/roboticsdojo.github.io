About Robotics Dojo

Welcome to the Robotics Dojo: an intensive robotics training program under the AFRICA-ai-JAPAN Project at the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT).

Through the project-based activity and training, the participants will be able to strengthen their ability to develop robots and conduct robotics research.

The two Robotics Dojo robotic competitions were held in October 2023 and October 2022 at JKUAT.
